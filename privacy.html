Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 February 2026

Aurora Wake Up ("we", "our", "the Service") is operated by Neon Golden Ltd. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Aurora Wake Up App Clip.

What We Collect

When you use Aurora Wake Up, we collect:

  • Device notification token — a unique identifier provided by Apple that allows us to send push notifications to your device. This is not linked to your identity.
  • Hotel identifier — the participating hotel where you scanned the QR code.
  • Consent timestamp — when you agreed to receive alerts.

What We Don't Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No browsing or usage analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data shared with third parties

How We Use Your Data

Your device token is used solely to send you aurora alert notifications on the night you sign up. We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than delivering the alert service.

Data Retention

All data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of your registration. Your notification permission is ephemeral (granted by Apple's App Clip framework) and expires automatically after 8 hours of inactivity.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) to deliver alerts. Apple's privacy policy governs their handling of push notification delivery. We do not use any other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Aurora Wake Up does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Service is designed for hotel guests and does not require any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
