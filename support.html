Aurora Wake Up — AI-powered aurora alerts for hotel guests

How It Works

Aurora Wake Up is an App Clip designed for hotel guests in Queenstown, New Zealand. Scan the QR code on the Aurora card in your hotel room, allow notifications, and go to sleep. If the Aurora Australis appears overnight, you'll receive a notification so you can step outside and see it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Aurora Wake Up?

Find the Aurora card on your hotel nightstand and scan the QR code with your iPhone camera. The App Clip will launch instantly — no download required. Tap "Get Started", allow notifications, confirm, and you're set.

Do I need to download an app?

No. Aurora Wake Up uses Apple's App Clip technology, which launches instantly from a QR code. There's nothing to install or keep on your phone.

Will the notification wake me up?

The notification will arrive like any other app notification. To make sure it wakes you, we recommend adding Aurora Wake Up to your Sleep Focus allow list in Settings, or keeping your phone on vibrate on your nightstand.

How long do notifications last?

App Clip notifications are ephemeral and expire automatically after 8 hours from your last interaction. No data is retained after that.

What data do you collect?

Only a device notification token (to send the alert), the hotel identifier, and your consent timestamp. No names, phone numbers, or personal information. Everything is deleted within 24 hours. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Which hotels support Aurora Wake Up?

Aurora Wake Up is available at participating hotels in the Queenstown region. Look for the Aurora card on your nightstand.

Does it work on Android?

Not yet. Aurora Wake Up currently supports iPhone only. An Android version is planned for the future.
